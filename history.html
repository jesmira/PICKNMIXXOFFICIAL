20th February 2000
STORE OPENING
In the early 2000, Tastefull was officially open, the launching was done by our first Chief Executive Officer (CEO), Mr Azam bin Syamsul at Jalan Telawi 2, Bangsar, Kuala Lumpur which is our old location. The opening was overwhelming and slowly people start to acknowleged the existence of Tastefull. Our focus was to provide main courses for our customers including for breakfast,lunch, and dinner.
4th JUNE 2006
REBRANDING
After about six years and a half running this business, Tastefull decided to rebrand the store from Tastefull to PicknMixx and jump into Food and Beverage industry. The rebranding also have change our services and our color theme. Fortunately, this rebranding received overwhelming support from the local and we also got to attract more customers to come to our store.
14th August 2010
NEW LOCATION
After two years of rebranding we successfully moved our location from Bangsar to our current location, Lorong Raja Chulan, Kuala Lumpur. This location is much spacecious and convinienent than our old location. In 2010, we also have added variety of food in our menu to attract more young generation to come to our store.
20th February 2020
20th BIRTHDAY
It's offificially our 20th aniversary! To show our gratitude to our customers and give back to the communities, PicknMixx has donated about 2000 of our signature dish to the public. We also have officially joined the 1% For Our Plannet to support finding of solution for our environment crisis.
