Drivethru Crew


Responsibilities


  1. Greeting customers as they arrive and making them feel welcome.
  2. Directing customers to their seats and the restroom, nswering questions about the menu, and making note of special requests, if required.
  3. Maintaining cleanliness standards by keeping the counters, tableware, and utensils clean.
  4. Replenishing food supplies when required.
  5. Presenting customers with the bill and processing payments
  6. Assembling and packaging takeaway orders.
  7. Reporting all damaged or faulty equipment to the kitchen manager.

Requirement


  • At least SPM Graduate.
  • Prior experience in a similar position.
  • Excellent listening and communication skills.
  • The ability to process bills and cash registers.
  • Capacity to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Ability to stand for extended periods.
