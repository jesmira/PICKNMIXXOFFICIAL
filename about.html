OUR BUSINESS

PicknMixx is an Food and Beverage Business that focus on serving its customers with the best meals throughout the day. We could say that PicknMixx started back in 2000, but the truth is, PicknMixx starts every day. Our business goal is to ensure all of our customers step out from our store in the best mood and satisfied with our service as well as the meal. This business not only focusing on gaining profits, but we also focus on creating relationship with our customers. PicknMixx provides career, training and development opportunities to our local people.

HALAL


Our Ingredients are 100% Halal Certified

PicknMixx adheres to the principles of Halal by ensuring that every operating process and every food and beverage served meets the requirements and demands set by the Malaysian Halal Certification Procedures Manual in order to fulfil our customers' trust to ensure high quality Halal food are being prepared well from central kitchen before going on the plate for our customers.
