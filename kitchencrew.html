Kitchen Crew


Responsibilities


  1. Cleaning all dishes, work stations, cooking equipment, and food storage areas in accordance with food safety regulations.
  2. Washing, chopping, shredding, and grating ingredients for subsequent use by the chef.
  3. Sweeping and mopping floors, as required.
  4. Storing ingredients according to prescribed food safety regulations.
  5. Cleaning trash cans and disposing refuse on a regular basis.
  6. Reporting all damaged or faulty equipment to the kitchen manager.

Requirement


  • At least SPM Graduate.
  • Prior experience in a similar position.
  • Ability to lift up to 50 pounds.
  • Capacity to work in a fast-paced environment.
  • Ability to stand for extended periods.
