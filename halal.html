OUR POLICY


Our ingredients including meat, seafood and sauces are all Halal-Certified. We are careful in all aspects to honour our Halal commitment in serving our customers, from sourcing raw ingredients, food preparation, food processing and hygienic standards.

OUR COMPLIANCE


Our Central Kitchen in Penang and all of our stores have been Halal Certified or are currently pending Halal Certification, in compliance with JAKIM's Halal Certification requirements.
  • Zero Alcohol Content In Our Beverages
  • All Suppliers and Ingredients Used are Halal-Certified
  • Food Preparation & Cooking Practice Adhere to Halal Food Regulations
